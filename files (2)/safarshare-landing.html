Why SafarShare

Built for India.
Designed for safety.

We solved the problems that make ride-sharing unreliable — cancellations, unverified drivers, and zero safety features.

🛡️
Verified Drivers

Every driver is verified with Aadhaar, driving license, and vehicle RC before being approved. Admin reviews each application manually.

🆘
Panic Button

One tap sends your GPS location to emergency contacts, our safety team, and optionally the nearest police station.

📍
Live Tracking

Real-time GPS tracking throughout your journey. Share your live location with family so they always know where you are.

❌
Anti-Cancellation

Drivers who cancel within 12 hours face rating penalties and temporary suspensions. We take reliability seriously.

💰
Save Up to 70%

Split fuel costs with co-travellers. Lucknow to Kanpur for ₹160 instead of ₹600 by taxi. Same journey, fraction of the cost.

💬
In-App Chat

Message your driver directly after booking. Share pickup details, locations, and coordinate without sharing personal numbers.
